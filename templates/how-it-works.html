HOW IT WORKS

Change the Temperature in a Zone

Comfort Zone allows you to customize every passenger’s environment with ease. Change your temperature, sound levels and more with the press of a button. Thanks to our zone management system, every zone in the car is customizable to tailor the needs of everyone inside.

Change the Environment Profile

Comfort Zone also ships with several premade profiles that adapt to a variety of weather conditions and situations. Instead of tuning the whole system every morning, pick a single profile that fits your needs at the time.

Add a new Environmental Mode

If none of the available profiles fit you, you are welcome to add your own. Whether you have frequent passengers or a specific way you like your car in the morning, you can save your options and reuse them at a moment’s notice.

Control Nearby Devices

Want to take customization to the next level? You can also adjust the devices connected to Comfort Zone. Any Android devices connected to the system can have their volumes adjusted. Whether you want some peace while driving or to kick the fun up a notch, you are in complete control.
