Who are we?

We are a group of seven students at the Software Engineering and Management program in Gothenburg University. In the fall semester of 2019 we were offered the chance to collaborate with APTIV on a proof of concept project.

Why did we make Comfort Zone?

Being comfortable in your car is essential, whether you're taking a short city drive on a hot summer day or driving across country on a cold winter night. With Comfort Zone you not only have a full overview of the interior environment of your car, but you have the ability to adjust temperature, sound levels, air pressure and so much more at a press of your fingertips.

Our Teams

Android Team

Our Android team was responsible for everything the customer sees on their dashboard.

Hardware/Backend Team

Our hardware and backend team put together the system which gathers the data and saves it to the database so that it can be used in the Android application.
